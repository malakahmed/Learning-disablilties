Causes of Learning disabilities Effects of learning disabilities
1-Heredity: Often, learning disabilities run in the family, so it’s not uncommon to find that people with learning disabilities have parents or other relatives with similar difficulties. 1- Learning disabilities causes troubles such as keeping words and letters in reading in order, spelling, and writing; keeping numbers in order in math; keeping track of a class schedule; completing long-range assignments, such as a term paper; and following directions.
2-Problems during pregnancy and birth such as injuries during or before birth;also because of low birth weight, lack of oxygen, drug and alcohol use during pregnancy, and premature or prolonged labor. 2-As a result they will have a memory deficit that can cause problems with taking notes in class; remembering homework assignments, spelling or vocabulary; recalling information studied for tests; remembering basic math facts, phone numbers, addresses, locker combinations, people’s names, appointments.
3-Incidents after birth such as head injuries, nutritional deprivation, and exposure to toxic substances (i.e. lead) can contribute to learning disabilities. 3-It can impact relationships with family, friends and in the workplace.
